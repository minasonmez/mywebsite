Hello World

An introduction — why I started writing, and what this blog is for.

Hi. Welcome to my corner of the internet. I've been meaning to start a blog for a while — one of those things that lives on the mental to-do list between "learn more about interpretability" and "read that paper from three months ago." But here we are, and here it is.

I'm not sure exactly what this space will be yet. Probably a mix: notes on things I'm studying, thoughts on research I find interesting, occasional reflections on being a student navigating the intersection of cognitive science and machine learning. Maybe some personal writing too, if the mood strikes.

I built this site mostly because I wanted somewhere that was mine — not a LinkedIn update, not a tweet, not a form submission into a void. Just a place to think out loud and leave a trace. If you stumbled here, hello. I hope you find something worth reading.
